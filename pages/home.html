Yu-Cheng Kuo (郭育成) 簡介

About Yu-Cheng Kuo.

  • I am Yu-Cheng Kuo, with a Master's degree in Information Management from National Sun Yat-sen University and a Bachelor's degree in Information Management from Cheng Shiu University.

    During my undergraduate studies, I assisted three professors in completing research projects funded by the Ministry of Science and Technology, participated in Industry-Academia collaboration projects at the Industrial Technology Research Institute (ITRI), attended academic conferences, and applied for patents. I graduated as the top student in my department.

    During my Master's studies, I joined Professor Wei-Po Lee's Artificial Intelligence Lab, served as a teaching assistant for the data structures course, and assisted in collaborative projects with Sun Yat-sen University and Kaohsiung Medical University. These projects primarily applied deep learning-based object detection techniques to automate dental X-ray image recognition. Additionally, my thesis focused on deep learning, image recognition, and model compression using knowledge distillation, titled "Multi-Teacher Knowledge Distillation Method On Self-Distillation Model Training."

    After graduation, I briefly worked as a research assistant at Kaohsiung Medical University while waiting for my military service. After completing my service, I joined the Industrial Technology Research Institute (ITRI) as an Associate Engineer, where I was responsible for developing AI medical diagnostic systems and sign language recognition systems. About a year and a half later, I transitioned to AUO's Intelligent Applications Department as a Senior Engineer, focusing on AI algorithm development. My current projects include pedestrian attribute recognition and pilot action recognition, among other AI research and applications.
